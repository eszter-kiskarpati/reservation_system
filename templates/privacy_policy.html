Privacy Policy

We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

1. Information We Collect

When you make a reservation using our website, we may collect the following information:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Reservation date and time
  • Party size
  • Any additional notes you choose to provide (e.g., allergies, special requests)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information only for the following purposes:

  • To process and manage your reservation
  • To contact you with confirmations, updates, or issues regarding your booking
  • To improve our services and internal processes

3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or trade your personal data. Your information may only be shared with the following:

  • Restaurant staff responsible for managing reservations
  • Service providers that help us send emails or SMS messages
  • Our hosting provider as part of normal website operation

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard practices. Only authorized staff members have access to reservation information.

5. Cookies

This website uses a basic session cookie required for website functionality. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies.

6. Your Rights

You may request at any time:

  • Access to the data we have about you
  • Correction of inaccuracies
  • Deletion of your personal information

To make such a request, please contact us using the information below.
